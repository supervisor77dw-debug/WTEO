About WTEO – World Trainer Elite Organisation

WTEO is the international authority for certified quality in the PMU industry. We establish global standards, promote professionalism, and enable experts to make excellence visible.

Our Mission

To certify Artists, Trainers and Academies at the highest level – officially verified, transparently documented and internationally recognized.

Our Vision

A global quality movement where competence, responsibility and integrity receive visible recognition.

Our Values

  • Excellence
  • Responsibility
  • Integrity
  • Transparency
  • International outreach

Why WTEO?

WTEO elevates the industry by awarding certification only to true top performers. Our certificates are a reliable proof of real expertise.
