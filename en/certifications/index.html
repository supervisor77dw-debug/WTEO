WTEO Certifications – The Official Quality Standard of the PMU Industry

The WTEO certification represents the highest form of recognition for PMU professionals worldwide. It makes excellence visible – officially evaluated, transparently documented, and internationally acknowledged.

Who is the certification for?

  • Artists with outstanding technical precision
  • Trainers who educate at the highest level
  • Academies that meet international quality standards
  • Professionals who lead rather than follow

The Three WTEO Certifications

1. World Elite Artist

Certification for PMU artists who demonstrate the highest level of technique, precision and aesthetics.

Requirements include:

  • full portfolio
  • qualification documents
  • hygiene certification
  • optional process videos

2. World Elite Trainer

For educators who shape the industry.

Requirements include:

  • teaching concepts
  • curriculum
  • educational materials
  • documented student results

3. World Elite Academy

For institutions offering world-class training.

Requirements include:

  • structured curriculum
  • educational materials
  • assessment processes
  • consistent educational quality

Certification Process

1

Submit application

Select certificate, fill out the form, submit initial details.

2

Upload documents

Portfolio, documents, credentials — checked via a GDPR-compliant system.

3

Individual evaluation

Evaluation is done individually by WTEO experts, not automated.

4

Final certification

Upon successful evaluation, you receive your official WTEO certificate, a unique ID, and entry into the World Elite Register.
